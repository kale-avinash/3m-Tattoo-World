A Permanent Tattoo is a lasting form of body art created by inserting ink into the deeper layers of the skin using a tattoo machine. It’s a powerful way to express your identity, beliefs, emotions, or memories through meaningful designs. At 3M Tattoo World Panchavati, we ensure a safe, hygienic, and professional tattooing experience using high-quality equipment and sterile techniques.

A Temporary Tattoo is a non-permanent form of body art that allows you to enjoy the look of a tattoo without the lifetime commitment. Made using skin-safe ink or stickers, these tattoos are perfect for events, festivals, photo shoots, or simply experimenting with designs before going permanent.

Permanent Tattoo
